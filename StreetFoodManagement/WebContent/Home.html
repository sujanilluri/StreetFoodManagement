Chicken Barbeque

Grill BBQ Chicken: Season your chicken with salt and pepper. Barbecue the chicken with the skin side down for about 15 minutes. Turn it over and baste with BBQ Chicken Sauce (make your own homemade barbecue sauce or use pre-made). Cook an additional 20 to 30 minutes, constantly basting with the sauce.

Roasted Chicken Wings
